Product Description

The Eicher 380 tractor is a reliable and budget-friendly choice for small to medium-sized farms. With its 40 HP engine and air-cooled system, it’s built for performance, economy, and low maintenance.

  • Strong 3-cylinder 40 HP engine
  • Suitable for plowing, hauling, and other farming tasks
  • Low fuel consumption and high performance
  • Easy to operate for new and experienced farmers
  • Ergonomic driver seat and easy controls
